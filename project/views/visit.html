Our Location

The incline is located at 1197 W Carson St, Pittsburgh, PA 15219. We are open 365 days a year!

Groups


We welcome groups of all sizes to the incline. We offer special discounted rates for groups of 10 or more. Please contact us for more information on group rates and self-guided tours.

Self-Guided Tours

Self-guided walks are a great way to explore the incline at your own pace. Our self-guided walks include the following:

  • A pictorial history of Pittsburgh's industry, inclines, sports venues, and natural disasters.
  • A visit to the machine room to observe the original hoisting equipment from 1877 in operation.
  • A panoramic view of Downtown Pittsburgh from the observation deck 400 feet above the three rivers.
  • Plan to allow time for photos and a visit to our gift shop for post cards and souvenirs.
